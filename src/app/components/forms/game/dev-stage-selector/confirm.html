When switching your game page from a %{ from } page to a %{ to } page, the follow changes will occur:

  • People will now be able to rate your game (unless you turn it off in your Settings).
  • Your early access ratings will be archived and we will show a separate rating average for your complete game page.
  • Your complete game ratings will be archived and we will show a separate rating average for your early access game page.
  • Ratings will be disabled for your game. Any ratings you currently have will be archived until you transition back to an early access or complete game page.
  • Public packages/builds for your game will now be visible to everyone.
  • Any public packages/builds for your game will be hidden while your game page is devlog-only.
  • Your game's devlog feed will be moved into its own tab. You can continue posting devlog posts for your game.
  • Your game's devlog feed will move back to your game's main page.
  • Your game will be added to the top of the "new game" list.
